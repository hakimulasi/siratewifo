    How Tall is Russ?

    As earlier mentioned, the 31 year-old American rapper is 5 feet 6 inches (1.68 m and 168 cm) tall. He can be categorized among American rappers/men whose heights are below the average height for American males, going by a 2018 report by the United States public health agency Center for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC).

    According to the report, the average height for male Americans aged 20 years and above is 5 feet 9 inches. Based on this, it is obvious that Russ’s height of 5 feet 6 inches is below average by 3 inches. However, despite being shorter than most male Americans, his  physique is one that is still admired by many.

    Russ’s Height Compared With Ed Sheeran

    Ed Sheeran is an American rapper famously known for his hit song The A-Team. He and Russ seem to share a lot lately in their careers as they have been spotted several times featuring each other in their music videos. But while they have a number of things in common, their height is not one of them.

    Ed measures 5 feet 8 inches in height and this makes him two inches taller than Russ. Although they are both below the average height for American males, judging by the CDC report mentioned earlier, Ed is only an inch shorter.

    Owing to the fact that the difference in their height is not much, the duo sometimes appear to be of the same height as seen in the video of Russ’s song titled Are You Entertained, which features Ed Sheeran.
