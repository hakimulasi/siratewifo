    Timeline

    Rowe was engaged to singer/actor Ashley Parker Angel. The couple broke up in 2008, after a five-year relationship. In 2006, Lynn gave birth to their son, Lyric Lennon Parker-Angel. Lyric is an actor who plays Lachlan Drake on American Horror Story: Hotel.
