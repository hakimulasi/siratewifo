    Harry B Biography

    Nigerian Awards winning actor as well as a top notch singer, Harry B was born on October 8, 1959, into a family that hail from Emene, Enugu state a South Eastern Part of Nigeria Predominated by the Igbo tribes. He is currently 61 years old. He was born in Mbaise, Imo State, Nigeria.

    He lived part of his early life in Enugu State, Nigeria, Where he attended Saint Patrick Secondary Emene, Enugu State before he left Nigeria in 1985, to further his education in the United States Of America. While Harry lived in the US, he developed interest in music before returning to Nigeria in 2004 to join Nollywood. The good thing abou Harry B is that he is both a musician and a an actor.
