    The king and queen have left the stage! Beyonce and Jay Z shared an emotional moment on Saturday, Sept. 13 during their final On the Run Tour concert in Paris, France. The couple, who have been touring together since June, took some time to thank one another as the crowd roared. 

    “I love you so much!” Beyonce, 33, said to her husband of six years, while wiping away tears and putting her hands over her heart. “Give it up for Mr. Carter!” 

    HOVA, 44, then added his own personal note, facing his wife and saying, “It’s been an honor and a pleasure to share the stage with you. I couldn’t dream of anything else than to be in a stadium with the woman I love, who I believe is the greatest entertainer of all time. Make some noise for Beyonce!”

    It was an emotional night for the pair, which was filmed for HBO’s highly-anticipated special and filled with surprises. Rapper Nicki Minaj took the stage with the “Flawless” singer to perform their remix to the female empowerment song. 

    “Tonight was their last show which made it even more special,” the “Anaconda” emcee captioned an Instagram photo with Beyonce. “Honored to have experienced this with two ppl who’ve influenced me more than they’ll ever know. Thank you BEY&JAY :)”

    The AP reports that Jay Z also told fans that the city was special to the couple because “We got engaged here and this is where baby Blue was conceived.”
