    The Meaning Behind the Song

    “El Cuerno De Chivo” is a popular song by Los Dareyes De La Sierra that tells the story of a powerful weapon and its deadly impact. The song begins with a description of the narrator visiting a friend in Aguascalientes, where he comes into possession of a weapon called “cuerno de chivo,” which translates to “goat’s horn” in English. The song emphasizes the destructive nature of this firearm, claiming that its bursts of fire are cursed, leaving no man alive.
