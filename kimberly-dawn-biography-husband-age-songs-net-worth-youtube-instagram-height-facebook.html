    Biography

    Kimberly Dawn (born April 7, 1974) is a multifaceted artist from the United States. She is a country musician, flight instructor, and entrepreneur who illuminates the country music landscape with her melodic prowess.

In 2019, she took flight into the music scene, releasing her debut single, The Bottle, as a gateway to an enchanting musical odyssey. Her musical journey continued to blossom with captivating releases like ‘93 and Stronger, captivating audiences across Canada and the United States through her beautiful performances.

    Beyond her musical endeavours, Kimberly Dawn‘s passions soar to new heights as a certified flight instructor, guiding aspiring aviators on their soaring quests. Embracing her role as a catalyst for change, she founded Advancing Aviation, a visionary non-profit organization that empowers women and girls through flight training opportunities. In this harmonious fusion of music and aviation, Kimberly Dawn epitomizes the spirit of empowerment, using her platform to inspire others to fearlessly chase their dreams.
