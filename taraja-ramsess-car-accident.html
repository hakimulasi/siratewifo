    Taraja Ramsess car accident details

    Famous stuntman Taraja Ramsess recently passed away, along with his two daughters, Sundari and Fugibo, due to an accident on October 31, 2023, at night.

    Ramsess and his daughters, Sundari, aged 13, and Fugibo, only 1 month old, tragically lost their lives in an instant when their car collided with a truck.

    Kisasi, aged 10, Shazia, aged 3, and another child are still in recovery while authorities investigate the cause of the crash, as reported by the police to the affiliate.

    As per WSB-TV, Taraja was driving his Ford F-150 towards Wesley Chapel Road when the collision happened around 11:53 p.m.

    He crashed into a damaged tractor-trailer parked on the road, which had three passengers inside.

    The Dupabin County Police Department stated that the victims are still in a serious condition.

    Moreover, the Chicago Police Department’s Traffic Specialist Unit is investigating the causes of the collision.
