    Charles Stanley Funeral Baptist Pastor Dead At 90

    14 Aug 2024, 00:00

    The Charles Stanley Funeral is expected to draw a large crowd of mourners who were impacted by his life and ministry.

    Dr. Charles Stanley, a beloved senior pastor at First Baptist Church Atlanta and founder of In Touch Ministries, passed away peacefully at his home on Tuesday morning at the age of 90. 

    Dr. Charles Stanley was a prominent Christian figure known for his unwavering faith, dedication, and love for God. 

    As the senior pastor of First Baptist Atlanta for over 51 years, he led the congregation through periods of growth and change, impacting the church’s mission and values.

    The details about The Charles Stanley Funeral are yet to be disclosed.

    Born in Dry Fork, Virginia, Stanley’s upbringing was marked by the loss of his father at a young age. Despite this hardship, he determinedly pursued his education, earning degrees from prestigious institutions such as the University of Richmond and Southwestern Theological Seminary.

    Stanley’s career as a pastor spanned several churches across the country, including Fruitland Baptist in North Carolina and First Baptist in Miami, Florida. 

    However, it was his time at First Baptist Atlanta where he truly made his mark, serving as an associate pastor before taking on the role of senior pastor in 1971.

    In addition to his pastoral duties, Stanley was also a prolific author and broadcaster, using his platform to spread his message of faith and hope to millions of people. 

    His books, including “The Gift of Forgiveness” and “How to Listen to God,” continue to inspire readers around the world.

    Throughout his tenure at First Baptist Atlanta, Stanley oversaw significant changes within the church, including its growth to over 15,000 members and its move to a new location in Dunwoody. 

    Despite his retirement in 2020, the congregation and the wider Christian community continued to feel his influence and leadership.

    In September 2020, Stanley transitioned to the role of pastor emeritus of First Baptist Atlanta after serving as senior pastor for half a century. 

    Even in his retirement, he continued to inspire and encourage people through his ministry.

    Dr. Charles Stanley’s passing is a great loss to the Christian community, but his life and legacy will continue to have a profound impact for years to come. 

    His unwavering faith, dedication, and love for God touched the hearts of countless people around the world. 

    As we mourn his passing, we can take comfort in knowing that he is now with his Lord and Savior, Jesus Christ.
