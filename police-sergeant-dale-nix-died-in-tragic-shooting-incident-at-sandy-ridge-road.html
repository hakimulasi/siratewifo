    In a shocking incident that has left the Greensboro community in mourning, off-duty police officer and watch commander Sergeant Dale Nix was killed in a shooting at the Sheetz on Sandy Ridge Road.

    According to reports, the deadly shooting occurred on Saturday night. The circumstances surrounding the incident are still under investigation, and further details are yet to be released by law enforcement officials.

    Sergeant Dale Nix was a respected member of the Greensboro Police Department, known for his dedication to duty and commitment to serving the local community. His sudden demise in such tragic circumstances has sent shockwaves through the department and the community he served.

    As the city grapples with this devastating loss, tributes have begun pouring in for Sergeant Dale Nix. Colleagues, friends, and community members are sharing their memories of the fallen officer, painting a picture of a dedicated public servant who will be deeply missed.

    Our thoughts and condolences go out to the family, friends, and colleagues of Sergeant Dale Nix during this incredibly difficult time.
