    Jose Canseco is a 6-time All-Star outfielder and designated hitter who was born on July 2, 1964 in Havana, Cuba. He is best known for his 462 career home runs and being named American League MVP in 1988 with the Oakland Athletics. Canseco has been a controversial figure in the baseball world, as he admitted to using performance enhancing drugs and his 2005 book called out numerous other big name players for using steroids.
