    'Marriage Story' was one of the successful movies of Noah Baumbach. The 2019 film also led him to the nomination of Best Motion Picture of the Year, Best Performance by an Actress in a Leading Role, and Best Performance by an Actor in a Leading Role in the Oscar. Though the awards did not come in his account, Laura Dern brought the Oscar for Best Performance by an Actress in a Supporting Role.

    Many relate the movie as the reflection of Noah's real-life experiences. And, he has admitted that he kept an emotional presence in writing the film along with the actors in their performances. 

    Marriage Story's plot is very much relevant to Noah's married life with his ex-wife, Jennifer Jason Leigh. They first met in 2005 and married on 3 September 2005 before the release of The Squid and the Whale, which was based on his parent's divorce.

    Noah Baumbach and his ex-wife, Jennifer Jason Leigh (©: Zimbio)

    Noah's understandable relationship was also seen in his professional career. He directed Jennifer in his movie Margot at the Wedding (2007) and Greenberg (2010).

    But in 2010, they started living separately, as of their tumultuous relationship. After three years of their split, they finalized their divorce in 2013. They have a son named Rohmer Emmanuel Baumbach (b. 17 March 2010).

    Noah's Relationship Details With His Girlfriend Greta Gerwig And Kids Details

    Noah Baumbach, age 50, is now rejoicing his romantic bliss with his fiancee, Greta Gerwig. The couple has also has worked together in movies like Frances Ha (2013), Mistress America (2015), and While we're Young (2015).

    Noah and Greta are together for nine years since they first met on the set of Greenberg. After Noah separated with his ex-wife, Jennifer, he moved on with the Little Women director.

    Noah Baumbach with his girlfriend, Greta Gerwig (©: Instagram/Noah Baumbach)

    However, it wasn't until 2018, when they publicly confronted their love life. Noah and Greta also displayed their love dimensions at the 2019 Oscars and Golden Globes. Later, on 19 March 2019, they welcomed their first kid named Harold, after which Vogue featured both Greta and Harold in their mag.

    Greta Gerwig and her son Harold (©: Twitter/Vogue Magazine)

    Despite their longterm relationship, the couple does not appear to be in haste for their Wedding. Noah and Greta appeared on Late Late Show with James Corden in January 2020, where they called each other 'husband' and 'wife.' During the show, Greta mentioned; 

    "Boyfriend makes it sound like I just met him last week. 'Lover' is disgusting. And 'fiancé' makes it sound like there's an imminent wedding. So none if it works."

    The lovebirds are yet to officiate their Wedding.

    Noah Baumbach; His Bio And Net Worth

    Noah Baumbach is one of the most prominent film directors of the American entertainment industry with well-acclaimed movies like Marriage Story (2019), Mistress America (2015), and Frances Ha (2012). 

    Noah was born on 3 September 1969 in Brooklyn, New York, in the United States. He is the son of Georgia Brown and Jonathan Baumbach, who are two figures of film critics. His father, Jonathan, is also a writer. Noah, who stands at the height of 5 feet and 9 inches tall, studied at Vassar College, and his first film, Kicking and Screaming (1995), was based on his academic life at college.

    Noah has written and directed numerous movies like Mr. Jealousy (1997), The Squid and the Whale (2005), Margot at the Wedding (2007), While We're Young (2014), The Meyerowitz Stories (2015), and Marriage Story (2019). Through his consistent career as a filmmaker, he has derived the net worth of $16 million.

    Besides that, Noah and his girlfriend, Greta Gerwig, has a combined net worth of $20 million, with $4 million in Greta's account.
