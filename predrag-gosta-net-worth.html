    Predrag Gosta Overview and Wiki

    Early Life and Musical Beginnings

    Predrag Gosta was born into a musically rich heritage in Belgrade. His path in music began early, fostered by diverse cultural influences from his Serbian-German father and Croatian mother. Gosta’s early exposure to music and performance led him to the Belgrade Music Academy, where he first honed his skills.

    Artistic Leadership and Direction

    Gosta’s leadership prowess is evident through his roles as Artistic Director of New Trinity Baroque, an esteemed early music ensemble, and the New Belgrade Opera, which he founded to focus on baroque opera.

    His direction extends to the Gwinnett Ballet Theatre in Atlanta, where he serves as Music Director. His role in these positions underscores his versatility and commitment to fostering classical arts in various forms.

    In 2012, Gosta returned as the Artistic Director of the Belgrade International Early Music Festival, an event he has helped grow into one of the largest and most successful of its kind in southern Europe.
