    Delonte’s basketball skills gained prominence during his time at Eleanor Roosevelt High School. He played a crucial role in the school’s team reaching the Maryland 4A championship. Despite losing, West received several honors and awards for his outstanding performance. He continued his basketball journey at Saint Joseph’s University, where he played alongside Jameer Nelson, contributing to the team’s remarkable season record and an Elite Eight NCAA Tournament appearance.

    Entering the 2004 NBA Draft after his junior year at Saint Joseph’s, he was chosen as the 24th overall pick by the Boston Celtics. Despite initial struggles due to injuries, West’s determination saw him excel in the subsequent years.
