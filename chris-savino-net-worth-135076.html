    About

    Animator and Writer known for creating the Nickelodeon show The Loud House and Bigfoot & Gray on the run. He's also worked on shows like Cow and Chicken, Dexter's Laboratory, and The Powerpuff Girls.

    Before Fame

    He became a fixture in the industry in the 1990s, landing jobs on shows like The Ren & Stimpy Show and Rocko's Modern Life.

    Trivia

    He's been nominated for Emmy Awards for his work on Kick Buttowski, Escape From Cluster Prime, and The Powerpuff Girls.

    Family Life

    He was born in Royal Oak, Michigan. He married Bethany Savino in 1999; they have three sons together.
