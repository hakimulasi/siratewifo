    Mahek Chahal Facts

  • She grew up in Norway.
  • She co-starred as Pooja Iyengar in the 2003 Bollywood comedy film, Nayee Padosan, which is partially inspired by the Tamil film Indru Poi Naalai Vaa and Chashme Buddoor.
  • Alongside Harbhajan Mann, Neeru Bajwa, and Kanwaljit Singh, Mahek Chahal made her Punjabi film debut by playing the role of Lisa Kaur in the drama film titled Dil Apna Punjabi (English: Our Heart is Punjabi) in 2006.
  • She appeared as Tia Roberts in the 2009 romantic film, Main Aurr Mrs Khanna, starring Salman Khan, Kareena Kapoor, Sohail Khan, and Bappi Lahiri.
  • In 2016, Mahek Chahal played the character of Manjulika Shah, Saudamini’s daughter and Shakti’s sister who is Rajbeer’s former lover, in the Colors TV supernatural horror drama series named Kavach… Kaali Shaktiyon Se.
