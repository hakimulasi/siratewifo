    Mario Lopez Jr is an American television host and actor, best known for his portrayal of AC Slater in Saved by the Bell. He has appeared in numerous television series, in films and on Broadway. Currently, Mario works as a host for the entertainment news magazine Extra. His first professional role was on the show, aka Pablo

    The multi-talented father of two has appeared on other shows such as Dancing with the Stars, America’s Best Dance Crew, The X Factor, and Pacific Blue. In addition to hosting television shows, Mario dances, acts and writes author’s books. He has been active in the entertainment industry since 1984, when he was eleven years old.

    In 2012, he co-hosted the second season of American version of The X Factor with Khloé Kardashian-Odom and the sole host of the third and final season. He also ran a reality show with his wife, Courtney Mazza.

    Biography (Age)

    Mario was born in San Diego, California on October 10, 1973 to Mario Michael Lopez Sr. and Elvia Lopez. Her mother was a telephone company employee and her father worked for the National City District. Lopez was raised in a large Catholic family of Mexican descent. He attended Chula Vista High School and graduated in 1991.

    The sensational star kicked off his career in the entertainment industry when he starred as Tomas’ younger brother on the ABC comedy series, aka Pablo. It didn’t last very long, and in the same year he played drums and a dancer on Kids Incorporated. Mario has since blazed a trail in his career, exploring the industry as much as possible. From movies to series to reality TV hosting, Mario clearly hasn’t slacked off. He started his own radio show, ON with Mario Lopez in 2012, for Premiere Radio. Mario has published three non-fiction books, one of which portrays him and his baby girl Gia.

    Net worth of Mario Lopez

    Mario has been in the entertainment industry for a long time, basically since he was a child, and not only is he really popular, he has also amassed a great amount of wealth. Mario Lopez is said to have a net worth of $16 million. He earns $3.5 million a year.

    Luxury is not an issue as Mario owns one beautiful and expensive home, estimated at $1.9 million. The house has a swimming pool, four bedrooms and six bathrooms. It is considered one of the best celebrity homes.

    Family – Wife, Daughter

    So far, Lopez has been married twice. His first marriage was to Ali Landry whom he dated for six years. They married in a Catholic ceremony in Mexico City and the wedding was annulled about two weeks later. In an interview, Lopez admitted their split came to a head when Ali found out he had cheated on her days before their wedding, at his bachelorette party. He had a few dates after her and remarried in 2012 to Broadway dancer, actress and producer Courtney Mazza. They had started dating in the fall of 2008. Together they have a daughter and a son, Gia Francesca, born in September 2010, and Dominic, born in September 2013.

    On September 27, 2011, Mario released a children’s book titled Mario and Baby Gia. The book is non-fiction and is about Mario and his infant daughter Gia.
