    Who is Bailey May?

    Bailey May is a Filipino actor, model, singer, plus T.V personality. He is extremely prominent for his role in “On the Wings of Love” (2015), “Maalaala mo kaya” (1991), and “Pinoy Big Brother” (2005).

    Bailey May: Professional Life, Career

    Bailey May started his acting career by giving auditioning as a housemate for Pinoy Big Brother: 737 and successfully get a chance to act in the show. In the same year, he made his film debut and appeared as ‘Zach Rodriguez’ in a film titled “Trending: Love.”

    His first music album “Bailey” came out in 2015. From 2015 to 2016, he acted as ‘Harry Fausto’ in a T.V show titled “On the Wings of Love”.

    He released his song titled “O Pag-ibig” from the ‘Himig Handog Pop Lovesong 2016’ album in the year 2016. The same album (Himig Handog PPop Lovesong) made him win four awards including ‘MYX Best Music Video Award’, ‘MOR Choice Awards, and ‘TFC Global Choice Award’.
